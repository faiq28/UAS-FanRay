Benefits of Branding & Content Creation

Here are 8 benefits of having a professional design for your business

01

As Business Identity

Business needs an identity to be recognized by people. This identity can be visualized through design. This design will later be included in various media, such as brochures, banners, websites, social media, and others.

02

Showing Business Professionalis

Apart from showing professionalism, a professional and attractive design has a big impalement in getting the attention of your potential business customers.

03

Increase Sales Balance

The more attractive a design is, the greater the chance for your product or business service to be chosen by prospective customers, that way our business can be known by many people so that it can increase the sales balance.

04

Big Impact First Impressions

Good design is not only attractive, but can also give the impression that is right on target, thus providing a different experience from its competitors.

05

Promotion & Sales Facilities

Good design is able to convey a message with its visuals. Promotion has become easier and potential customers can get to know our business through well-delivered messages

06

Differentiation creates Trust

A good design can help our potential business customers with similar businesses. A professional design helps a business gain the trust of potential customers.

07

Loyalty Earning Tool

People find it easier to trust products with designs that look professional. Thus, our business has a greater chance of gaining their loyalty.

08

Unlock opportunities to increase prices

After getting loyalty, we can increase the selling price of products or services. In this phase, people do not only buy products, but also buy brands that are represented by professional designs.

FAQ About Manufacturing Services

What is a website?

A website is a collection of pages that help you promote online. Can help you to market your product, service or brand more easily.

What are the advantages of having a website?

In the era of the industrial revolution 4.0 where almost everything has used the internet, having a website is an opportunity for business people who want to improve and achieve more opportunities. Both from the medium to upper business scale to even the smallest.

Can I sell and make transactions with the website?

Yes, you can sell and make transactions through the website easily. You can start by selecting some of the available website themes according to your needs. FanRay also provides several guidelines for managing your product through the website.

Can my website be accessed anywhere, anytime, and not only via a computer?

Yes, you can access your website through any device. All the themes that FanRay provides are mobile-friendly, so that the appearance of your website can be seen optimally on any device.
