How to Order

Website Development Process at FanRay

4 easy steps you need to know when ordering a Website / Application with our services.

01

Order Website

After you have decided that you want to create a website that suits your business (Website Company Profile, Website Sales, E-commerce, etc.), you can immediately place an order by contacting our Customer Service.

02

Website Creation Planning

After you order, please pay a down payment according to the invoice that was issued. Next, plan the structure of your website content / you can consult with us so that the appearance of the content structure can suit your needs.

03

Website Making

Our team consisting of project managers, graphic designers and developers will create a temporary page so you can see the results and provide design revisions before publishing.

04

Publish Websites
