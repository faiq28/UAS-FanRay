FAQ About Manufacturing Services

How much does it cost to create a website or android application?

We determine the costs for making an android, iOS or website application according to the features desired by the customer and the time needed to work on the project. You will get a price quote after our team has checked and discussed the web or application you want.

What development skills does FanRay have?

As long as it's within the scope of the website and the Android platform, we can develop websites/applications in any field.

What needs to be prepared in developing an application or website?

On average, what needs to be prepared to create a website or application is a clear and complete description of the application / website you want to make, budget, date line, content in the form of manuscripts/text and images, as well as the server. For more information, please visit our website. The data provided by the customer will affect the time and cost of developing an application or website.

Can my website be accessed anywhere, anytime, and not only via a computer?

Yes, you can access your website through any device. All the themes that FanRay provides are mobile-friendly, so that the appearance of your website can be seen optimally on any device.
